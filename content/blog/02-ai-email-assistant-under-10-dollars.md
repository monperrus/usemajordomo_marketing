## What About Privacy?

This is the #1 question about AI email tools.

**Here's what you need to know:**

**All AI email tools must:**
- Read your emails (that's how they work)
- Send email content to AI models (OpenAI, Anthropic, etc.)
- Store some data for learning/improvement

**What to look for:**
- SOC2 Type II compliance
- GDPR compliance
- Clear privacy policy
- Data encryption
- Option to delete your data

**Questions to ask:**
1. Where is my email data stored?
2. Is it used to train public AI models?
3. Can I delete all my data?
4. Who has access to my emails?

Majordomo, Gmail AI, and Superhuman all meet basic security standards. Free tools (like raw ChatGPT) do not.

**Pro tip:** Never use AI for emails containing:
- Social Security numbers
- Credit card details
- Medical information
- Legal documents
- Passwords or access codes

Set up filters to exclude these from AI processing.
