- Send Day 0 email Mon-Thu, 9am-11am recipient timezone

- Never send weekends
- Stop sequence if reply received

**Personalization tokens required:**
- {{firstName}} - First name

- {{industry}} - Their industry (for relevance)

- Test 2-3 subject lines per email
